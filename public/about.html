About IR7 Football Shop

Your trusted partner for premium football gear in Bangladesh since 2020

Our Mission

To provide football enthusiasts in Bangladesh with access to high-quality, affordable football gear while promoting the sport and supporting local talent development.

  • Quality products at fair prices
  • Exceptional customer service
  • Fast and reliable delivery
  • Supporting local football communities

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a group of passionate football players, IR7 started as a small local shop in Dhaka with a simple goal: make quality football equipment accessible to everyone.

What began as a humble storefront has grown into Bangladesh's premier online football shop, serving thousands of satisfied customers nationwide.

We understand the needs of football players because we are football players ourselves. Every product in our catalog is tested and approved by our team.
