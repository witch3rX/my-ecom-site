Return & Refund Policy

Hassle-free returns and quick refunds for your peace of mind

30-Day Returns

Return any unused item within 30 days of purchase for a full refund or exchange.

Free Returns

Free return shipping for defective or incorrect items.

Quick Refunds

Refunds processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your return.

Return Process

1. Request Return

Contact our customer service within 30 days of receiving your order to initiate a return.

2. Package Items

Pack the item in its original packaging with all tags and labels attached.

3. Ship Back

Use the provided return label or ship to our return address.

4. Receive Refund

Once we receive and inspect the item, we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days.

Return Eligibility

Items That Can Be Returned

Unused items with original tags and packaging within 30 days

Non-Returnable Items

Used or damaged items, personalized/custom products

Exchange Policy

We offer free exchanges for:

  • Wrong size received
  • Defective products
  • Wrong item shipped

Exchanges are processed within 24 hours of receiving the returned item.
